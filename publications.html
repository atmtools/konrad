Publications#

Here is a list of publications that used konrad. If you are aware of any publications that are currently not listed just open an issue or pull request.

BSKJdlC21

Jiawei Bao, Bjorn Stevens, Lukas Kluft, and Diego Jiménez-de-la-Cuesta. Changes in the tropical lapse rate due to entrainment and their impact on climate sensitivity. Geophysical Research Letters, 48(18):e2021GL094969, 2021. doi:https://doi.org/10.1029/2021GL094969.

BKS21

Stella Bourdin, Lukas Kluft, and Bjorn Stevens. Dependence of climate sensitivity on the given distribution of relative humidity. Geophysical Research Letters, 48(8):e2021GL092462, 2021. doi:https://doi.org/10.1029/2021GL092462.

DKS+19

Sally Dacie, Lukas Kluft, Hauke Schmidt, Bjorn Stevens, Stefan A. Buehler, Peer J. Nowack, Simone Dietmüller, N. Luke Abraham, and Thomas Birner. A 1d rce study of factors affecting the tropical tropopause layer and surface climate. Journal of Climate, 32(20):6769 – 6782, 2019. doi:10.1175/JCLI-D-18-0778.1.

KDB+21

Lukas Kluft, Sally Dacie, Manfred Brath, Stefan A. Buehler, and Bjorn Stevens. Temperature-dependence of the clear-sky feedback in radiative-convective equilibrium. Geophysical Research Letters, 48(22):e2021GL094649, 2021. doi:https://doi.org/10.1029/2021GL094649.

KDB+19

Lukas Kluft, Sally Dacie, Stefan A. Buehler, Hauke Schmidt, and Bjorn Stevens. Re-examining the first climate models: climate sensitivity of a modern radiative–convective equilibrium model. Journal of Climate, 32(23):8111–8125, 2019. doi:10.1175/JCLI-D-18-0774.1.

KDA+21

C. A. Kroll, S. Dacie, A. Azoulay, H. Schmidt, and C. Timmreck. The impact of volcanic eruptions of different magnitude on stratospheric water vapor in the tropics. Atmospheric Chemistry and Physics, 21(8):6565–6591, 2021. doi:10.5194/acp-21-6565-2021.
